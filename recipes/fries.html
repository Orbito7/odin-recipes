Fries

Description

Indulge in the crispy perfection of seasoned French fries! These golden delights, tossed in a tantalizing blend of herbs and spices, offer a burst of flavor with every crunchy bite. Whether served as a side or enjoyed as a snack, these fries are a crowd-pleaser that never disappoints. Whether baked to a golden crispness or fried to perfection, they're the perfect accompaniment to any meal. Elevate your fry game with this simple yet irresistible recipe that's sure to leave taste buds tingling for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2lbs russet potatoes peeled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp garlic salt
  • 1 tsp onion salt
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup water

Steps

  1. Cut potatoes into french fries and place into a large bowl of cold water

  2. Preheat oil to 350F in a large saucepan

  3. Prepare batter while oil is preheating, combine lour, garlic salt, onion salt, paprika, and salt togtehr into a large bowl. Add a little water to make the batter a bit thick.

  4. Dip fries into the batter made after they are patted dry.

  5. Fry until golden brown by dipping the fries in the oil in small batches

  6. Drain the fries onto a plate that has paper towel on top

  7. Enjoy!
