Chicken Burger

Description

Savor the deliciousness of homemade chicken burgers! These juicy patties, seasoned to perfection, are nestled in soft buns, offering a burst of flavor in every bite. Whether grilled, pan-seared, or baked, they're a wholesome and satisfying meal for any occasion. Top with fresh lettuce, pickles, onions, and your favorite condiments for a classic burger experience with a twist!

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken Breasts
  • 3/4 cup Buttermilk
  • 1 tbsp Siracha
  • 3/4 cup Flour
  • 1 tbsp Baking Powder
  • 2 liters Oil

  • Spices

    • 1 tbsp Salt
    • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
    • 1 tbsp Yellow Mustard Powder
    • 1 1/2 tbsp Dried Herbs
    • 1 1/2 tbsp Onion Powder
    • 1/2 tbsp Paprika
    • 1/2 tbsp Ground White Pepper

  • Toppings

    • Buns
    • Lettuce
    • Pickles
    • Onions
    • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Chicken

    1. Prepare the chicken, flatten to an even thickness

    2. Marinade, mix togther the Buttermilk, Siracha, Salt, Garlic Powder in a large enough bowl that can hold the chicken breasts. Place the chicken inside the marinade, and mix well. Cover and chil this for 4 hours.

    3. Frying, heat 2 liters of vegetable oil in a large sauce pan to 350F (use a thermometer as necessary). After oil is up to tempeture, slowly lower the chiken into it and cook for 3 minutes on side and then cook for another 2. After the cooking process transfer the chicken to a wire rack with a paper towel wrapped sheet tray underneath.

  2. Assembly

    1. Toast burger buns on a pan with butter till golden brown.

    2. Cut vegetables and Prepare condiments

    3. Spread your sauce on both buns and put the chicken breast on top of that. Then put your vegetables.

    4. Enjoy!
